What is Investment?

In general, an investment is an asset or item accrued with the goal of generating income or recognition. Investment can also help us in wealth creation. When you invest, you buy assets that you expect to increase in value over time, which can grow your money significantly. This will lead us to a concept called passive income, in which these assets that you have invested will grow over time and give you money without doing anything.
