INVESTMENT
What is Investment?
In general, an investment is an asset or item accrued with the goal of generating income or recognition. Investment can also help us in wealth creation. When you invest, you buy assets that you expect to increase in value over time, which can grow your amount of money.
Where Should I Invest?
Gold is a precious metal that are used in a lot of things. For example, jewelry, electronics and even in medicine. Because of this, gold is rare and very valuable.
Stock is a concept of buying a certain company's share of ownership. If you buy a stock, it means that you own a certain amount of percentage of that company.
Property refers to houses, apartments, lands, etc. This investment has been done for generations and many people have achieved financial freedom because of it.
