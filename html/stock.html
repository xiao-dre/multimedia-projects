Stock

What is Stock?

Stock (often called equity) is a document or security that represents the ownership of a certain percentage of a company. This means that the owner of the stock have a right over some of the company's assets and earnings proportional to the owner's quantity of shares.

Why Invest in Stock?

Stock is worth to invest because of it's high return potential. If you found the next big company or just invest in a big company now, you can expect a great return in a few years. This is because company like these will always be growing and with them growing, so will your share's value. Plus, you will be given a share of the company's profits by the form of dividend. This way, you will have a passive income as a way to achieve financial freedom.

Some Tips on Picking

Great Stocks

01/

Pick a stock that are from a renowned indexes such as LQ46 and KOMPAS100. These stocks that are inside the indexes have a healthy liquidity and usually are big companies.

02/

Pick a stock that are categorized as a blue chip company or has a big market cap. By picking these stocks, you don't have to worry about the value dropping anytime soon because of their big market caps.

03/

Pick a company that have a healthy financials. Every quarter of the year and anually, public companies must release a financial report, you can read this report and see if it is healthy or not.
