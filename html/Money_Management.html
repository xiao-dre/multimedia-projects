Money Management
The flow of money is often neglegted by individuals. How much you earn and how much you spend is important to achieve financial freedom. Managing this earn and spend cycle is called money management.
Money management is done by first knowing how much you spend your money and then make a budget. By making a budget, you will be able to allocate some of your earnings to your savings. This act of saving will help you buy important assets so that you will have something to make money without doing anything.
